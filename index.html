About Me

I'm Richard Fleming, I am an army veteran and student at devCodeCamp studying to be a full stack engineer. I studied computer technology at Midlands Technical College in Columbia, SC and earned an Associate in Science as well as an application programming certificate.

After completing my associates I began doing freelance work updating content on local business websites. I took a detour to learn about cyber security earning my Comptia Security+ certificate and worked a 6 month contract as a network analyst.

While I had secured a position in the IT field I decided I wanted to get more into the programming side and enrolled in devCodeCamp's Full Stack Engineer boot camp.

I am currently looking for my first Junior Full Stack Engineer position.
